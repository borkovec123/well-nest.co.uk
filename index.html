Why Overnight Heat Works

There’s strong clinical support for the benefits of consistent, gentle heat overnight — especially for those managing chronic joint conditions like arthritis. Heat increases blood flow to stiff joints and tight muscles, easing inflammation and improving mobility.

During the night, body temperature naturally drops, which can intensify stiffness in the morning. Using a heated underblanket helps maintain an optimal warmth level while you rest, preventing joints from seizing up. Many users report feeling looser, lighter, and more mobile the moment they rise.
